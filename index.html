About Us

Sahayogi Secondary School is a well-established educational institution located in Gokarneshwor-4, Kathmandu. Dedicated to fostering academic excellence and holistic development, the school serves as a nurturing environment for students from diverse backgrounds.

History and Establishment

Sahayogi Secondary School was founded with the vision of offering affordable and accessible education to the local community. Over the years, it has grown from a small establishment to a full-fledged secondary school, gaining recognition for its dedication to academic standards and community involvement.

Academic Programs

The school offers comprehensive educational programs from primary to secondary levels, adhering to the national curriculum of Nepal. At the secondary level, it provides specialized courses in Social Studies and Population Studies, equipping students with critical thinking skills and a deeper understanding of societal issues.

Extracurricular Activities

In addition to academic excellence, Sahayogi Secondary School places significant importance on extracurricular activities. It regularly organizes cultural programs, sports events, and educational workshops to ensure the all-round development of students.
